Welcome!

My name is Clark, and I'm in the third year of my bachelors in mechatronics engineering at UBC! I'm particularly interested in controls engineering, and the interesection of mechanical, electrical, and software design.

For the last year, I've been working with professors in UBC's mechanical engineering department to improve and streamline lab content, but I'm also involved in many other areas of the UBC community. I'm on the frames and enclosures subteam of UBC Subbots, and I'm one of the social coordinators for the mechanical engineering undergraduate student council, Club MECH.
